Private Lessons

Private, 1:1 lessons that allow direct focus with one student. This format is great for children learning how to swim for the first time. Students will use lesson time to practice various strokes and learn about water safety measures.

Family Lesson

Families are always welcome to join us at Mermaids & Tadpoles. Whether you're watching or participating, having the support of a parent nearby is valuable for children who may be timid around the water. These lessons are an excellent way to bond with your family in a fun, safe environment.

Group Swim

Swimming with your friends can be tons of fun. Group lessons offer swimmers the chance to make new friends and develop new skills.

Water Safety Course

The Water Safety course is designed to keep swimmers safe by teaching them the rules of the pool. Ettiquite, general safety measures, and emergency protocols will all be taught over the duration of the course.
